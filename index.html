How it works

Three steps, one network

AMAN is built to be simple to join and genuinely useful from day one.

01

Join the directory

Add your name, industry, location, and graduation year. You're searchable in minutes.

02

Share what you know

03

Mentor the next class

What we offer

Built around three pillars

More than a directory — a structured network with purpose at every level.

Alumni directory

Searchable by industry, location, and graduation year. Find a fellow graduate wherever your career takes you.

Referral web

Job leads, client referrals, and hiring opportunities shared within a circle of graduates you already trust.

Mentorship pipeline

Students connect with working alumni for career clarity, informational interviews, and real-world guidance.
